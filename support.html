Restoring a purchase

If you've upgraded and it's not showing on a new device or after reinstalling, open the Upgrade screen in the app and use the restore option — purchases are tied to your Apple ID, not to a device.

How stories work

Your child picks a story world, a character, and the weather, then fills in the missing words to create a one-of-a-kind story. No two stories are ever quite the same.
