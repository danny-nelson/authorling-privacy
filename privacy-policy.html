Authorling — Privacy Policy

Last updated 1 July 2026

What we collect

Authorling doesn't require an account. Your child's name, age group, and favourite colour are stored only on your device (in local app storage) so the app can personalise stories. That information never leaves your device and we never see it.

Purchases

If you choose to upgrade, the purchase is processed by Apple/Google and RevenueCat, our subscription infrastructure provider. RevenueCat receives an anonymous purchase identifier to confirm your entitlement — it does not receive your name or any profile information.

No ads, no tracking

Authorling has no advertising, no third-party analytics, and does not track you across other apps or websites.

Third parties

We do not sell or share any data with third parties, other than the purchase processing described above.

Children's privacy

Authorling is designed for families and children. Because all profile data stays on-device and no account is required, we do not collect personal information from children. Purchases require a grown-up to solve a confirmation step before they can proceed.
